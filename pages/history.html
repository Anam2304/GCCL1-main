HISTORY

In the year 1965, a single entrepreneurship business house named ‘Auto Equipment’, the flagship enterprise, started toddling in the business arena with agriculture related business like import and distribution of low-lift irrigation pump & shallow tube-well and a repair workshop. After a long journey of more than 5 decades, we are, at present, a multi-disciplinary business conglomerate engaged in Distribution and Manufacturing type of business under a single umbrella named Group QA with a staff strength of around 8000 people. At our credit, we have a successful track-record of dealing in crop protection chemicals, agricultural hybrid seeds, automotive spares and accessories, power tools and equipments, agricultural machinery, readymade garments and consumer items.
